About this website

This toolkit was created to help users with disabilities learn more about the job search process and find resources that can aid in career development. It was made for students of Georgia Tech Excel, a certificate program for individuals with disabilities, but most of the information is relevant to all residents of Georgia. To learn more about the program, visit the official Excel page.
