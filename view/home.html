This Toolkit is designed to help graduates of post-secondary education programs for students with developmental and intellectual disabilities connect to resources and agencies that will help them secure employment opportunities in the Atlanta, Georgia area as well as throughout the state of Georgia. The resources in this Toolkit will also be useful to other students and adults with disabilities that are looking for a job.

Toolkit contains local, state and national resources that will help you link to a job, decide what type of work is a good fit for you, complete job applications, make or update your resume, start your own business, apply for jobs; including employment with the federal government.
